Information Systems and Technology

The Digital Business Program (S1) prepares graduates with excellent digipreneur skills and the ability to develop and apply proficient digital technology. Students will be equipped with theory-based learning, practical experiences, and modern classroom facilities to ensure optimal learning experiences. The Digital Business Program has three main concentrations: Digital Marketing, Technopreneurship, and Digital Business Analyst.

Vision:

To become a Program that Produces Competent, Skilled, and Excellent Digipreneurs Nationally and Globally in Accordance with Christian Values.

Mission:

  • Conducting Higher Education based on Digital Business Science and Technology to Produce Competent, Skilled, and Excellent Graduates Globally in Accordance with Christian Values.
  • Conducting Research to contribute to all external and internal stakeholders of the Digital Business Program.
  • Implementing Community Service that Provides Significant Value and Impact in accordance with Digital Business Science.
  • Establishing Cooperation with Government Institutions and Business/Industry Sectors in the field of Digital Business.

Targets:

  • Implementing higher education activities in accordance with National Higher Education Standards to produce skilled and competent graduates in the digital business sector.
  • Conducting quality research activities with innovative methods relevant to current digital business issues.
  • Impact on society through the application of digital business science in solving social and business problems in society.
  • Initiating cooperation with potential partners from relevant agencies and business/industry sectors that can help develop the digital business program.

Career Prospects for Graduates:

Digital Marketing:

  • Copywriter or Content Writer
  • PPC (Pay-per-Click) Specialist
  • Social Media Marketing (SMM)
  • Email Marketing Specialist
  • SEO Specialist
  • KOL (Key Opinion Leader) Specialist

Technopreneur:

  • Startup Owner
  • Edupreneur
  • E-Commerce
  • IT Agency

Digital Business Analyst:

  • Data Scientist
  • Business Consultant
  • Trend Marketing Data Analyst
  • Content Analyst
