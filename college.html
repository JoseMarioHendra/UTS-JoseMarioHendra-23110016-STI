Institute Technology and Business Sabda Setia

  • About College

    • History

      • On October 13 2019, the Yayasan Gereja Protestan Kampung Bali (YGPKB) established a Higher Education section with the aim of forming a higher education institution called the Sabda Setia Institute of Technology and Business (ITBSS). After going through a long process, Decree of the Minister of Education, Culture, Research and Technology Number 460/E/O/2021 concerning Permit to Establish ITBSS in Pontianak City, West Kalimantan, was released on November 11 2021.

  • Vision & Mission

    • Vision of ITBSS

      A Higher Education Institution grounded in Christian values, preparing excellent human resources.

    • Mission of ITBSS

      To educate and train individuals with character, skilled, ready to compete globally, and bring a positive impact to their surroundings.
