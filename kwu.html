Entrepreneurship

The Entrepreneurship Program (S1) aims to prepare students to become Creativepreneurs who can identify and apply new ideas, concepts, and strategies in planning, managing, and developing a business or enterprise.

Students will be challenged to discover and respond to opportunities in entrepreneurship. The Entrepreneurship Program trains students to be sensitive and independent in becoming reliable entrepreneurs or business owners.

Career Prospects for Graduates:

  • Entrepreneur
  • Business developer
  • Business Analyst
  • Marketing Analyst
  • Marketing Specialist
  • Finance manager or auditor
  • Business Consultant
  • Online business manager
