Information Systems and Technology

Information Systems and Technology Program is a combination of Information Systems (IS) and Information Technology (IT) to address the challenges of rapid technological development and digital transformation. IST studies how to design and implement digital products (applications, websites, and designs) that align with business goals and solve everyday life problems. IST also explores emerging technologies.

Vision of the Information Systems and Technology Program

To become a leading program in the field of Information Systems and Technology, especially in software engineering, and to contribute to the development of new technologies based on Christian values and technopreneurial spirit.

Mission of the Information Systems and Technology Program

  • Conducting education and teaching to produce quality graduates in the field of information systems and technology that meet the needs of society and industry.
  • Conducting research to develop quality knowledge and technology.
  • Organizing community service activities in the field of Information Systems and Technology.
  • Establishing cooperation between the Information Systems and Technology Program and relevant institutions and industries, nationally and internationally.
  • Fostering the character of students by instilling Christian values, integrity, work ethic, and sense of responsibility, thus preparing them to become successful and contributing members of society.

Goals of the Information Systems and Technology Program

  • Producing graduates with a technopreneurial spirit who utilize information systems and technology to innovate and solve real-world problems.
  • Preparing graduates to be innovative technology experts centered on users who can develop and implement effective, efficient, and tailored technology solutions according to industry needs.
  • Equipping graduates with the necessary competencies to perform their job tasks effectively and efficiently, in line with industry requirements.
  • Enhancing research activities in the field of information systems and technology as a contribution to technological development.
  • Increasing the results and impact of community service in the field of information systems and technology that are beneficial to society.

Targets of the Information Systems and Technology Program

  • Augmented & Virtual Reality Developer
  • Data Analyst & Scientist
  • Digital Transformation Specialists
  • Internet of Things Specialists
  • IT Project Manager
  • Network Security Analyst
  • Software Developer
  • UI/UX Designer
